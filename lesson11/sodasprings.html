Local Article

"Don't Drink the Water"

By: Dusty Bench

Many local residents will be happy to hear that the town council has come to an agreement to naturally flavor the naturally occuring carbonated water in Soda Springs.

While details have not been released regarding the flavor the council has agreed to use, the decision is expected to increase tourist activity to the area.

The changes are expected to take effect Spring of next year.
